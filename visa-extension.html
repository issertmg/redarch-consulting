Visa Extension

You must complete an application to extend your stay in Canada as a student. First, you must check the expiration date of your current permit and make sure to apply before the expiration date. You must renew your visa/permit at least 30 days before your current status expires. Then, if you decide to change the program, school or institution, you must apply to change the condition of your current permit.
