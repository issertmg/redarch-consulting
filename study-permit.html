Requirements needed for a Canada student visa:

  • An Acceptance letter from a Designated Learning Institution (DLI) in Canada.
  • You must prove that you have enough proof of funds to pay for your tuition fees and living expenses for you and all the family members who came with you to Canada.
  • You should be a law-abiding citizen of your country; you must not have a criminal record and be a security risk to Canada. You must provide a police clearance from the territory or country where you have been residing for the past ten years.
  • You must be in good health, would not cause health risks to Canada, and must be willing to undergo a medical examination if necessary.
  • You must prove to the immigration officer that you will leave Canada at the end of your authorized period of stay.
