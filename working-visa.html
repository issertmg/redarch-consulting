Who are eligible for an Open Work permit?

  • Spouse or common-law partner of a temporary foreign worker
  • Spouse/common-law partners of an International Students
  • International Students who recently graduated from a Canadian post-secondary institution and are eligible for the Post-Graduation Work Permit Program
  • International Experience Canada (IEC) Candidates (Working Holiday)
